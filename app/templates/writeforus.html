Write For Us

Would you like to write for us about science and technology? At In Wait of Tomorrow we’re constantly looking to grow our team. We are a news and blog website that write mainly about science and technology, we work hard to try and write content that people enjoy and that we enjoy talking about.

Our website is coded, designed and developed completely from scratch, we have a following on Instagram, Facebook and Twitter as well as an email newsletter! With all the hard work put into maintaining the website and social media pages, there’s less time for the actual writing, which is why we would love more people to contribute! If you’d like to join our team and become a regular writer, or publish a guest post, we would love to hear from you. You can choose your own content - the topics we and our fans like the most include (but not limited to):

  • Environment & Conservation
  • Biotechnology
  • Science Fiction film/tv/book articles
  • Healthcare
  • Technology & AI

Why write for us?

It’s a great thing to do in your spare time, if you’ve got a message you want people to hear and you want to build up a following, join us! If you write for us, you’ll get your own Author page, with a link that you can share with friends, family and also include in your CV and any applications. The page will contain your own short description, links to your chosen social media profiles and a list of all the articles you’ve written.

If you’re interested, drop us an email at inwaitoftomorrow@gmail.com, or use the contact form below!
